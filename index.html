Meet Dr. Trisha Bailey

About Dr. Trisha Bailey

Rising from the challenges of her Jamaican childhood, Dr. Trisha Bailey is now celebrated as the wealthiest Jamaican woman, a symbol of tenacity and empowerment. Her philanthropy shines in her substantial donations to the University of Connecticut, highlighting her Huskies legacy and dedication to advancing education. Having overcome a memory-erasing coma, Dr. Bailey channeled her adversity into Bailey’s Medical Equipment and Supplies, providing crucial medical resources. A mother of five and the owner of 46 pharmacies, her business acumen extends to owning stakes in NBA teams. Her story, marked by a commitment to healthcare and female empowerment, motivates many to unlock their potential and aspire to new heights.

Dr. Bailey’s Journey

A reflection on the milestones and achievements that mark her legacy.

  • 1990
    Beginning a New Chapter
    Moved to the US, embarking on a transformative journey that laid the foundation for future endeavors in education and activism.
  • 1999
    Academic Excellence
    Graduated from the University of Connecticut, a stepping stone towards advanced education and leadership in organizational development.
  • 2005 & 2010
    Continuing Education
    Earned an MBA and a Ph.D. in Organizational Leadership, furthering her commitment to education and professional growth.
  • 2022
    Philanthropic Impact
    Recognized for her generous contributions to the University of Connecticut, and her efforts to improve education and development in Jamaica.
  • 2023
    Unbroken: A Story of Triumph
    Published her memoir, sharing her inspiring journey of overcoming adversity to become a leading figure in philanthropy and education activism.
